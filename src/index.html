Welcome to Ting Hao

Ting Hao offers Chinese cuisine and has been open in Salisbury for over 20 years. The location is conveniently located in a shopping center, making it an excellent place to order take out or to dine in. Ting Hao is known for its great value on lunch specials and chef specialties.

Ting Hao currently only does take out orders right now due to the corona virus. When things are back to normal we will offer dine in again.
